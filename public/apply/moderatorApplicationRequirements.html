Moderator Eligibility Requirements

There are a few requirements you must meet in order to have your application considered

  • Minimum 20 hours of time spent in the server

    This is to ensure you are relatively dedicated to our server and not just some random person trying to get staff.

    You can check how much time you've spent on the server here.

  • No one else can use your Steam account

    This is for obvious reasons, if you get a staff position, we're giving it to YOU, not your friends, family, etc. Please change your password to keep others out if necessary.

  • Positive reputation

    If you have warnings on the server for breaking rules, had arguments with a staff member or anything like that, your application will most likely be denied.

  • Willing to add us on Steam (Skype and Discord strongly recommended)

    We need a way to contact you! If you cannot add us on Steam then we apologize, but you can't be accepted.

    Skype and Discord are currently our primary methods of communication. We use them to keep the staff informed on what's going on. Highly recommended but not required.

You Can Only Apply Once

Let's be perfectly clear about this. Once you apply, there is no going back! You get one shot and if you're denied, that's it, you don't get another chance. So make this one count! Make sure you have the requirements listed above and feel free to ask any of the staff if we think you should apply or not.
